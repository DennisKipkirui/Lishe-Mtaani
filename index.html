Lishe Mtaani is an initiative founded by three farmers from Nakuru and Kiambu County. We take interest in planting and partnering with farmers who produce quality products with assistance from known and established agronomists to cater for healthier living in growing and processing farm fresh consumables. Our aim is to bridge quality, value and convenience at the comfort of your home. Why go to the market for your grocery shopping when we can deliver the farm to you at your convenience.

Who we are

Lishe Mtaani is your immediate doorstep grocery shop. We are an online convenience store for all your grocery needs where you can experience value for your money, at your convenience and the best quality products in the market. We do not just promise, we deliver.

What do we do

Lishe Mtaani eases your life by removing the hustle of you creating time off your busy schedules to go to the market to source for your groceries. We source, package and deliver to your doorstep all your grocery requirements. We source these directly from farmers to ensure that quality is not compromised.

Why do we do it

With the help of research, We have noted that for the roduce to get from the farmer to the consumer, it passes a lengthy process because of the middlemen who more often than not will compromise on the quality and quantity. We at Keekapu Grocers are trying to bridge that gap by eliminating and shortening the process and thus gets the consumer the best there is to offer in the market. We have partnered with farmers and farms that produce quality fresh produce both for local and export markets.

How Does it Work

We Source

We strive to source as much as possible directly from farms in parts of Nakuru and Kiambu county. Our quality controls ensures that we stick to the best standards and practices in food safety and quality in far level production.

We Pack

We package the products after checking the quality of the vegetables and fruits

We Deliver

We deliver bags to different collection points accross Nairobi area on different days upon customer's order request. Any time we are called upon, we will be there, with our products.We strive to be consistent and reliable in our service delivery.
